U.S. Consumer Insights

Data-Informed Decisions

We analyzed prestige beauty purchases, social sentiment, and skincare habits across major metropolitan hubs to tailor our offering.

  • Skin Inclusivity: U.S. consumers expect shade diversity. Our complexion range launches with 40+ foundations and custom undertones for deeper skin.
  • Hybrid Beauty Demand: 62% of surveyed consumers seek multipurpose skincare-makeup products, inspiring adaptive textures and skin-care benefits.
  • Tech-enabled Service: Virtual artistry appointments allow national reach while AI shade-matching helps shoppers online and in-store.
  • Values & Transparency: Sustainable packaging, cruelty-free formulas, and ethical sourcing are table stakes for our target audience.
