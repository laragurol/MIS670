Sustainability & Ethics

Charlotte Tilbury believes in beauty that doesn’t compromise the planet. Our sustainability program focuses on reducing waste, using responsibly sourced ingredients and empowering our community to make conscious choices. Here’s how we are making a difference:

Refillable & Recyclable

Many of our products feature refillable packaging to reduce waste, such as the Airbrush Bronzer and our lipstick casings. Where refills are not possible, we prioritize recyclable materials and minimal packaging.

Cruelty‑Free & Vegan Options

All Charlotte Tilbury products are cruelty‑free. We offer a broad selection of vegan formulas for customers who wish to avoid animal‑derived ingredients. Our U.S. expansion maintains these commitments and ensures compliance with local regulations.

Responsible Sourcing

We work with suppliers who share our values on fair labour and environmental stewardship. We strive to use sustainably sourced mica and palm oil derivatives and continue to increase the proportion of renewable ingredients in our formulas.
